🎵 The 7 Basic Notes

The musical alphabet has just 7 notes: C D E F G A B — then it repeats.

On a keyboard these are the white keys. Tap each to hear it:

C — Do
D — Re
E — Mi
F — Fa
G — Sol
A — La
B — Ti

⬛ Black Keys — Sharps & Flats

Black keys sit between white keys. Each has two names: a sharp (♯) and a flat (♭).

C♯ / D♭
D♯ / E♭
F♯ / G♭
G♯ / A♭
A♯ / B♭

💡 How to find C on any keyboard

Look for a group of 2 black keys. The white key just to their LEFT is always C. From C, just go alphabetically: C D E F G A B C…

🔢 Octaves — why notes repeat

After B comes another C — but it sounds higher. This set of 12 notes (7 white + 5 black) is called an octave. The piano has about 7 octaves. Most songs live in 2-3 octaves.

🎹 What is a Chord?

A chord is 3 or more notes played at the same time. They work together to create a feeling — happy, sad, tense, or peaceful.

⚡ Major vs Minor

Major chords (C, F, G, D, A, E…) sound happy and bright.

Minor chords (Am, Dm, Em, Bm…) sound emotional or sad.

Tamil Christian songs mix both to create depth and worship feeling.

🌟 The 6 Chords that play MOST songs

These chords are all in the key of C major. Learn these 6 and you can play 80% of Tamil Christian songs! Tap each to see the keys:

C — Happy
Dm — Gentle sad
Em — Emotional
F — Warm
G — Tension/lift
Am — Deep worship

🎶 Most Common Song Progressions

Am → F → C → G — Very common in worship songs

C → Am → F → G — Happy praise songs

G → Em → C → D — Tamil Christian classics

Practice switching between these 4 chords smoothly — that's the goal!

📅 4-Week Beginner Plan

1
Week 1: Play C D E F G A B up and down every day. Find all the C notes on the keyboard.
2
Week 2: Learn C, Am, F, G chords. Practice switching between them slowly without stopping.
3
Week 3: Play a simple song using C Am F G. Open a song in this app and follow the chord names!
4
Week 4: Add Dm, Em, G7. Now you can play 95% of Tamil Christian songs!

✋ Finger Numbers

Fingers are numbered 1–5 (thumb = 1, pinky = 5).

C Major chord (right hand): Thumb (1) on C · Middle finger (3) on E · Pinky (5) on G

Am chord (right hand): Thumb (1) on A · Middle (3) on C · Pinky (5) on E

Left hand: Just play the root note (single key) — C for C chord, A for Am, G for G, etc.

💡 Tips that actually help

• Sit straight, keep wrists relaxed, curl fingers gently

• Practice slowly — speed comes later. Accuracy first!

• Open a Tamil Christian song in this app → look at the chord names → play those chords in order

• The progression Am → F → C → G works for dozens of songs — practice just this every day

• Use the Chord Practice section above to learn where each chord is on the keyboard

🎹 Quick Chord Formula

Major chord: Root + skip 3 white keys + skip 2 white keys
Example: C major = C, skip D, land on E, skip F, land on G

Minor chord: Root + skip 2 white keys + skip 3 white keys
Example: Am = A, skip B, land on C, skip D, land on E
